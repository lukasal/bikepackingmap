What is bikepacking map?

Bikepacking map uses your tracking data to create visualizations of multi-day trips like the one displayed on the right hand side. It shows the whole route in a way similar to the maps from Tour de France route visualizations including stage towns and the finish/depart. Additionally it shows all your stats from the stages as popups and can handle different activity types. These maps consist of:

  • The route based on GPS data
  • A "grand depart" marker at the start of the trip
  • A "grand arrivee" marker at the end of the trip
  • Markers for "stage locations" at the end of each stage
  • Different map types to choose from
  • Handling multiple sports types within one map using different colors for each type
  • Popups with altimetry and statistics shown by clicking on a stage route
  • All markers, styles and colors are fully customisable.
